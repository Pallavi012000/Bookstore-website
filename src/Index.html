What our Reader's Say

Here are some reviews given by our readers on Book Of Ideas by Brad Nu

Brain Woods

Book of Ideas was a very pleasant surprise to me. I expected this to be a sort of cold list of case studies, possibly self-promoting and self-referencing. But what the author has achieved here is a very good piece of storytelling: never overly endorsing himself for his achievements and the range of clients

Allen Agnes

A fantastically written book of one man's journey, experience and ideas of what it takes to follow one's passions for creativity and collaboration in the world of graphic design. The numerous and varied examples are more than enough to get the grey matter ticking and the creative juices flowing. I would highly recommend to anyone really.

Amiel Barbara

The Book of Ideas is part portfolio, part journal of one designer's experiences and lessons in the industry. As someone who lost their interest in design because of 'cheap and quick' outputs, this book rekindled my enthusiasm for design, in particular book design. This book is truly beautiful, and serves as a potent reminder that design is everywhere.

Olivia Louis

As a freelance designer, I found the material in this book to be invaluable. The showcase of Malinic’s design work is inspiring. However, the true gems of inspiration are found in the snippets of practical advice he’s able to divulge based on his extensive career in the creative industry. Malinic reminds creatives to face their fears, continue learning and most importantly – to find happiness in their work.
